4601YCOM Computer Systems

7 Segment Display

Logic circuit probably took up half of the entire module for computer systems but it is my preferred half. In my personal humble opinion, there is a really thin line between stress and fun when it comes to assembling a logic circuit. It is really fun when you have a clear vision of how the circuit would look in the end but when you do not, the wires can quickly get messy. Therefore, it is really good to keep in mind that a proper planning is needed when you have to do a logic circuit. Some of the methods that can be implemented in design planning are simplifying boolean expressions and making truth tables. This project really helped me understand the concept of logic circuit as it required a lot of researching and going through trial and error.

This project was assigned to me and two of my friends during the third semester of my foundation. Overall, it is a good experience working with my friends who carried out the analysis part of this research.
